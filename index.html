Features
Designed for the whole paddock
🗂️

Multi-file / multi-stint

Drop a whole day of sessions. Label drivers. Give two files the same name to merge a multi-stint driver.

🎬

Lap player

Animate any lap around the track with live speed and g-forces. Compare laps side-by-side, or scrub frame-by-frame.

🔥

Speed heatmap

Full-circuit speed heatmap with auto-numbered corners, drawn straight from GPS position data.

🏁

Catalyst-style coaching

Each corner: your real line vs. the fastest through there. Plain-language suggestions — brake later, carry more apex speed, get to throttle sooner.

⏱️

Where's my lap time?

Time-loss opportunities ranked by seconds available. Per-turn breakdown of exactly where the gap is.

📊

Stint statistics

Best, mean, median, std-dev, CV%, P95/P99 per stint. Caution-lap filtering built in.

🧩

Ideal lap

Composite best lap = fastest sector from anyone, stitched together. Each driver's theoretical personal best, too.

⚡

Fast parsing

Dependency-free JavaScript parser. A 17 MB file parses in ~50 ms. No server, no build step, no waiting.

🔒

Your data never leaves your machine

The analyzer is a single self-contained HTML file. Everything — parsing, analysis, all visualizations — runs locally in your browser. There is no backend, no account, and no upload of any kind.

How it works
Drop files. Get data.
1

Open the analyzer

Click "Open Analyzer" above — or download and open the HTML file directly from disk. Works offline.

2

Drop your .xrk files

Drag one session or a whole day's worth. The browser parses the binary format client-side in milliseconds.

3

Label drivers (optional)

Assign names to files. Match stints across sessions. The ideal lap pulls the best sector from everyone.

4

Analyze

Explore the heatmap, player, and coaching view. Find your biggest opportunities and go faster next session.
